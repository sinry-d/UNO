<h1 style="color: #5e9ca0; text-align: center;"><span style="color: #008080;">Welcome to the game of UNO!</span></h1>
<h3 style="color: #5e9ca0; text-align: left;"><span style="color: #000000;">Players: 2&nbsp;</span>&nbsp;</h3>
<p><strong><span style="color: #008080;">RULES:</span> Each player will be dealt 7 cards.</strong><strong> Players will take&nbsp;</strong><strong>turns playing one card at a time.&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong>The card played must be the same colour (same tens place) or the same&nbsp;</strong><strong>number </strong></p>
<p><strong>(same ones place) as the previous card laid down. If a player has </strong><strong>no cards that can be played,</strong></p>
<p><strong>they must draw a card from the pile. A player&nbsp;</strong><strong>can have a maximum of 20 cards in their hand. </strong></p>
<p><strong>Beyond 20, </strong><strong>they are not&nbsp;</strong><strong>required to draw. The first player to play all their cards wins. </strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>To <strong>PLAY</strong> a card: Type the number of the card into the console and press ENTER</p>
<p>To <strong>DRAW</strong> a card: Type the word "draw" into the console and press ENTER</p>
